Dimes

Overview

Dimes was created to ease the stress and intimidation that comes from finances and budgeting. Our easy-to-use platform and engaging design make the path to financial literacy enjoyable and gratifying.

Objective

Dimes is a native iOS web application designed to provide an enjoyable and upbeat approach to organizing finances, managing budgets, and growing savings.

Challenge

The biggest challenge in designing this finance app was presenting the necessary information while also evoking a professional, yet welcoming tone to users beginning their financial management journey. The process began with researching apps that are already on the market.
