ARTLO.

Overview

The idea for ARTLO. was born from my challenges selling work and supporting local businesses. I wanted to expand my circle and knowledge of local creatives to include musicians, shops, and other makers, to uplift the many artists and talents in my backyard.

ARTLO. is a platform to showcase any cityâ€™s unique voice through the people who live there and the work they put into their art, music, events, and businesses.

Objective

ARTLO. is a native application designed to connect consumers to artists and small businesses in their community. By selecting a specific city, users can browse visual art, blog posts, and local events on a vibrant, inviting interface.

Process

Research: I studied designs from Etsy, Craiglist, ASOS, and Depop to desseminate the most important features, layouts, and flows for users.

Sketches to Designing: I sketched forms with pencil and paper for some ideas and wireframes as a jumping point. After reiterating these sketches into digital medium fidelity wireframes, I proceeded with some testing, receiving feedback from potential users. and later refined the designs to high-fidelity wireframes.

Role

UX/UI Designer

Key Features

  • Location selection
  • Multimedia platform - blogs, articles, products/shops
  • Ability to access product posts, reviews, and receipts
