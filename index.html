RFRM

With an open-ended design brief and a new-found interest in social and political issues, I designed RFRM as an educational resource for users to access podcasts, news articles, and books on one platform.

The process for the native iOS and Android application involved a competitor analysis, user flow diagrams, multiple wireframe iterations, user testing, and peer critiques to create a user-centered design.

ARTLO.

The idea for ARTLO. was born from challenges selling work and supporting local businesses. I wanted to expand my circle and knowledge of local creatives to include musicians, shops, and other makers, to uplift the many artists and talents in my backyard.

ARTLO. is a platform to showcase any city’s unique voice through the people who live there and the work they put into their art, music, events, and businesses.
